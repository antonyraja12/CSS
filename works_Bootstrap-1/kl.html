K.L RAHUL

Kannanur Lokesh Rahul (born 18 April 1992) is an Indian international cricketer who currently is the vice-captain of the Indian cricket team. He is a Right-handed batter and occasional wicketkeeper.[b] He plays for Karnataka in domestic cricket and captains Lucknow Super Giants in the Indian Premier League.
